Note: somtimes there are blocking squares which we will explain later. The goal of the game is to have more pieces at the end of the game than your opponent. Ok now without further ado, the rules:
1. Any piece can move to any empty empty square so long as they clone their piece and make it 1 square away from an alied piece or moving any of your pieces two squares 2. The piece just now moved makes all adjacent pieces their color. adjacent meaning like a chess king's move 3. The game ends when all squares are full or nobody has moves Thanks for reading!
